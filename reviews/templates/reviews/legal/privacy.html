Privacy Policy

Last updated: {{ now|date:"F d, Y" }}

WorkInside (“we”, “our”, “us”) operates an independent platform where hotel employees share anonymous workplace experiences to help others make informed decisions. We are fully committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring compliance with applicable data protection laws, including the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

1. Information We Collect

We collect the minimum amount of personal data required to operate the platform, including:

  • Email address (used only for review verification — never shown publicly).
  • Review content voluntarily submitted by users.
  • Anonymous metadata (browser type, device, approximate location).
  • Cookies for basic functionality and analytics.

2. What We Never Collect

  • No full names
  • No phone numbers
  • No personal IDs or employment documents
  • No employer contact information
  • No sensitive personal data unless you voluntarily include it in a review

3. Why We Collect Your Email

Your email is used strictly to confirm the legitimacy of your review. After validation, your email is never displayed and never shared with hotels or third parties.

4. Legal Basis Under GDPR

We process your data based on:

  • Consent — when submitting a review.
  • Legitimate Interest — ensuring platform integrity and preventing abuse.

5. Data Retention

Emails are retained only as long as necessary for verification and security purposes. Reviews remain publicly visible unless you manually request removal.

6. Your Rights

  • Right to access your data
  • Right to rectification
  • Right to deletion (“right to be forgotten”)
  • Right to object to processing
  • Right to withdraw consent

7. Data Protection & Security

We apply industry-standard encryption and security practices. Only authorized personnel have access to backend systems.

8. Third-Party Services

We may use services such as email delivery (Mailtrap or similar) strictly for operational purposes.

9. Contact Us

For privacy questions or data deletion requests, contact:
workinside.contact@gmail.com
